Justice League: Crisis on Two Earths

Justice League: Crisis on Two Earths was an american animated film directed by Lauren Montgomery and Sam Liu, and written by Dwayne Mcduffie. this film was based and directly referenced to a vidion game called Justice League: Worlds Collide, it served as a bridge between Dc Animated Universe Series, Justice League And Justice League Unlimited. This Movie serves as the seventh film of the DC Universe Animated Original Movies.
